Hello!

 I’m Ruffa— but you can call me RM!
I’m a passionate and creative digital designer from San Juan City, Philippines. I thrive on crafting visually compelling designs and continuously push my boundaries to learn and grow.

Waste Analysis and Characteriation Study (WACS)

The City Government of San Juan through City Environment and Natural Resources Office conducted Waste Analysis and Characterization Study. It is about the study of the characterization and identification of solid wastes generated at source. I am in charge of the design and layout of all this IECs materials that was given to all participants including brochures, tarpaulins and certificates.
